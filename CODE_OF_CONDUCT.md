# Code of Conduct (flyover gpx)

## Our Pledge
We want participation in this project to be a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We aim to foster an open, welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be kind, respectful, and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate thoughtfully.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on improving the project.
- Respect boundaries and different viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Threats, violence, or encouraging harm.
- Publishing private information (doxxing) without explicit permission.
- Any other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, commits, wikis, and community channels) and to public spaces when someone is representing the project.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:
**bbarinka@gmail.com**

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as fairly and confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate to address violations, including:
- Requesting changes or an apology
- Removing content (comments, commits, issues, PRs, etc.)
- Temporary restrictions on participation
- Permanent ban from the project community
